News and Updates

This is the place where you will find information about what is happening in the parks. This page will get updated constantly, as all the parks seem to have changes daily.

Disney World

Epcot has added a new attraction: Guardians of the Galaxy. This is an indoor roller coaster with a backwards launch.

Guardians of the Galaxy

EPCOT

The new Galactic Star Cruiser Resort has been taking guests on a trip that's out of this galaxy. You will be transported . . .You have quests that are totally immersive.

Univesal Orlando

Universal will be adding new escape rooms to it's City Walk area. They are themed to Back to the Future and Jurassic World.

HHN is still going through the end of October. This frightful event takes place within the Universal Studios Park. It is a separate ticketed event.

Sea World

Sea World has added a new roller coaster in 2022 called Ice Breaker. They have plans to build another roller coaster to open in 2023. This new surf roller coaster will be located near the front entrance between front gate and Bayside Stadium. The name of the coaster is Pipeline: the Surf Coaster

Pipeline: the Surf Coaster

Coming soon to Sea World Orlando

Gatorland

Gatorland has been impacted heavily by Hurricane Ian. However, with a lot of work the team was able to open Gatorland again to the public. Their operating hours are: 10 AM - 5 PM every day.They have a trick or treat event on the weekends through October 30. This event, called Gators, Ghosts, and Goblins is fun for the whole family. It's included with park admission.

Busch Gardens

Busch Gardens recently opened it's newly redesigned Iron Gwazi roller coaster. It is considered to be the most thrilling roller coaster in FL.

Iron Gwazi at Busch Gardens Tampa Bay

Howl-O-Scream continues through the end of October. This seasonal event is sure to entertain as well as scare those that dare enter Busch Gardens after dark. Again, Howl-O-Scream is a separate ticketed event.

Legoland

Legoland has opened a new theme park right outside it's door. They named it Peppa Pig. It's designed with even younger ones in mind.

Fun Spot

Fun Spot has 2 theme parks in the Orlando area. One is located near Universal Orlando Resort in Orlando, FL and another is in Kissimmee, FL. They have trick-or-treat trail Saturdays and Sundays in October from 4 - 6 PM.
